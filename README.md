## Insightsüöò
- The base price of Lamborghini cars ranges between 200000 to 590000 USD.
- Most of the horsepower in the Lamborghini cars are around 650 hp
- Black color Lamborghini cars are the most sold in every year from 2020 to 2025.
- White color stays in the second place for the most sold Lamborghini cars.
- The sales volume of Americas and EMEA regions denote that most of the sales volume of Americas and EMEA lies variably between 1500 to 4800.
- Lamborghini Urus is the most sold model which is around 55000 between the years 2020 and 2025.
- Lamborghini Temerario takes the last place in the ranking with the sales of around 4000 cars.
- Gasoline cars are the most sold Lamborghinis which is above 100000.
- The average price of Lamborghini cars run by gasoline is lower than the Lamborghini cars run by hybrid engine.
- Lamborghini Urus ranks top as the most sold car running on gasoline with the number of cars around 55000.
- Lamborghini Urus SE tops as the most sold car running on hybrid engine with the number of cars sold between 27000 to 29000.
- The sales of Lamborghini Aventador has dipped less than 500 number of cars in the year 2023.
- Lamborghini Urus is the most sold car model by ranking in the first place between 2020 and 2023.
- From 2024 onwards, Lamborghini Urus SE has taken the first place as the most sold car.
- Urus tops as the most sold car in Americas region between 2020 to 2025 with more than 20000 cars.
- The sales of Lamborghini Urus, Lamborghini Urus SE, and Lamborghini Hurac√°n is high in the Americas region.
- Lamborghini Revuelto got the highest horsepower of 1000 hp.
- Lamborghini Temerario holds the second place for the car model with the highest horsepower having around 850 hp.
- Aventador is the costliest model of Lamborghini cars having a base price of nearly 590000 USD.
- Red ranks in the last place among the top 5 car colors sold by Lamborghini.
- Green color lamborghini cars the least sold car colors between 2020 to 2025.
